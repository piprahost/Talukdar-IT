# Shop Setup: Computer Accessories, Used Laptops & Laptop Service

This ERP is set up for a **computer accessories and used laptop shop** that also provides **laptop servicing**. The tagline can be: *"All types of computer accessories • Sales & Service • You Can Buy And Replace Here"*.

---

## 1. Shop identity

- **Settings → Company Info**: Set **Shop / Company Name** (e.g. "Computer Accessories & Used Laptops") and **Tagline** (e.g. "All types of computer accessories • Sales & Service" or "You Can Buy And Replace Here"). These appear in the sidebar and on documents.
- **Service Center Name**: Used on laptop service memos.

---

## 2. One barcode = one stock unit

The system is built so that **each physical unit has its own barcode**. For example, 10 laptop chargers in stock = 10 unique barcodes.

- **Receiving stock (Purchase or Manual entry)**  
  When you receive items, add or scan **one barcode per unit**. Each barcode increases stock by 1 for that product.

- **Selling**  
  When creating an invoice, **scan the barcode** of the unit being sold. One scan = one unit; the barcode is recorded and removed from stock.

- **Manual stock entry**  
  **Stock & Barcode → Add by Barcode**: Select product, then scan or type barcodes. Each barcode = 1 unit. Duplicate barcodes are ignored.

- **Product list**  
  For products that have barcodes, the **Stock** column shows the number of barcodes (traceable units). A "barcode" label under the number indicates 1 barcode = 1 unit.

- **Returns**  
  Sale/purchase returns can put a barcode back into stock (one barcode = one unit returned).

## 3. Sidebar order (daily use)

1. **Dashboard**
2. **Sales** – Invoices, Customers (buy/sell)
3. **Laptop Service** – Service orders (repair/servicing)
4. **Purchases** – Purchase orders, Suppliers (stock in)
5. **Payments**, **Expenses**
6. **Products & Stock** – Products, **Stock & Barcode**, Categories, Brands, Models
7. **Warranty**, **Returns**, **Accounting**, **Reports**, **Settings**, **Access Control**

---

## 4. Quick actions (dashboard)

- New Sale / Invoice  
- New Purchase Order  
- New Service Order  
- Add Expense, Payments  
- Low Stock, **Stock & Barcode**, **Add Stock by Barcode** (if permission allowed)  
- Sales Report  

---

## 5. Best practice

- **Assign a unique barcode to every unit** when receiving (purchase or manual entry).
- **Sell by scanning barcode** on the invoice so the correct unit is deducted and warranty can be tied to that barcode.
- Use **Laptop Service** for repair jobs; link warranty to barcode/sale when relevant.
